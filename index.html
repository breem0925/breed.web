BIOGRAPHY

広島県東広島市安芸津町生まれ在住。トラックメイク、ループJAMセッション、DJを一人で行う。周波数や元素を表現し、cymaticsや実験音楽、オーディオビジュアル作品を制作。現在、機材集めやアルバム制作中。

Born and residing in AkiTsu Town, Higashi-Hiroshima City, Hiroshima Prefecture. Engaged in track making and loop JAM session DJ alone. Expressing frequencies and elements, involved in making cymatics, experimental music, audio-visual works, and music activities. Currently collecting necessary equipment and producing an album.
